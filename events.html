Decoding ML 2.0

On the days 15th, 16th,17th of March, Meta developer circles organised Decoding ML 2.0 in continuation to the first Decoding ML 1.0 session with the aim to educate students of all years on the basic and essential concepts of machine learning. Day 1 of the workshop started of with a recap of the concepts covered in Decoding ML 1.0 followed by understanding of Pandas Profile, Plotting of graphs, Feature Engineering and its types. Day 2 covered types of machine learning - Supervised learning, Unsupervised learning along with their types followed by Hands-On projects where participants were provided with data sets and collectively worked on a Linear Regression model and another KNN model. On Day 3, Simple Linear Regression Evaluation Metrics, Cross-validation, Confusion Matrix including F1 Score, Precision and Recall were discussed. The audience were addressed by Retd. Cmdr. Gurumoorthy Gangadharan Sir. Certificates and Prices were distributed by him along with Vikas B Sir to the winners of the quiz and the members of MDC along with the speakers.

Time

Day1 - 3:00-5:00 , Day2 - 3:00-5:00 , Day3 - 3:00-5:00

Venue

VDC Bootcamp Hall

Meta Spark AR

On 17th February 2023, MDC GITAM Visakhapatnam organised an event based on AR and fundamentals of developing a filter or animations on Meta applications such as Facebook and Instagram. The event begins with the PR team greeting participants and introducing the evening's keynote speaker, Ms.Chhavi Garg. The speaker discusses new technologies such as AI, AR, VR, and so on. The speaker discusses the topic of the event. The team and speaker guided the participants through various technicalities and issues faced by the attendees. The event's hands-on session begins after the attendees have become familiarized with the fundamentals. Attendees are given the resources they need for the hands-on. Participants developed a custom filter/animation and submitted their work for review to Reskill.

Decoding ML 1.0

On the days of 9th and 10th of February, Meta developer circles organized an event on Machine learning which aimed to educate students of all years on the concepts of machine learning techniques, stages, models and much more with relatable examples for utmost understanding. The workshop started off with an insightful and informative speech by Mr.Vikas Sir, Deputy Director of Tech GCGC. Followed by an interactive session which was carried out by the key speakers Sourit Maji, vice president and Harshitha Kolipaka, core member of MDC Gitam, Visakhapatnam. Topics of Introduction, evolution, challenges of Machine learning, Machine Learning Development Life Cycle ,EDA and Hands-On were covered. The audience actively participated and majorly contributed to the success of the event.

Time

Day1 - 4:00-5:00 , Day2 - 3:00-5:00

Venue
